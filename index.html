*Rules for the Prevention of Infectious Diseases
• Wash your hands meticulously with soap under your palm and fingernails!
• Cover your sleeve when you cough!
• Coughing and other respiratory symptoms must wear a mask when visiting a medical institution
• Inform the medical team of their ability to travel abroad when visiting the medical institution
• Consult a local health center or 1339, area code+120, when suspected infectious diseases are reported.
